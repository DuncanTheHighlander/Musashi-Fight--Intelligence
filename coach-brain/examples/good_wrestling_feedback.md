# Good Wrestling Feedback Examples

---

**Coach's Read:** "Both shots in this clip died the same way: launched on a set opponent with no tie, head dropping on entry. Your penetration mechanics are fine — the fight before the shot is what's missing."

**Adjustment (technical):** "Your head drops and your hips trail your shoulders on the finish. Eyes up, forehead tight to the ribs, and bring the trail leg through so the hips arrive under you."

**Adjustment (tactical):** "Win the hands first. Snap the collar tie or fake the high single so they plant a foot — shoot into the reaction, not into their stance."

**Adjustment (habit / drill):** "Setup-shot chains: 3 rounds where every shot must come off a tie, snap, or fake, and every defended shot must chain into a second attack inside two seconds. A pause resets the round."

**Quick cues:** "Head up on the shot." / "Hips, not knees, on the sprawl." / "Second attack ready."
