# Good BJJ / Grappling Feedback Examples

Note how these stay at the positional level when limbs are occluded, and never invent grips.

---

**Coach's Read:** "You passed the guard twice and kept neither. Both times you chased the arm before the hips were dead, and both times they re-guarded through the space you left. The passing is ahead of the control."

**Adjustment (technical):** "In side control bottom you're flat with no inside frame — the cross-face is free and your hips are pinned. Near forearm across the neckline, get the shoulders angled, then shrimp onto the hip."

**Adjustment (tactical):** "Settle before you hunt: after clearing the legs, kill the near hip and control the far shoulder for a full beat before attacking. Position first — the arm will still be there."

**Adjustment (habit / drill):** "Pass-and-settle sparring: passes only count if you hold chest-to-chest control for three seconds before any attack. Re-guard resets the round."

**Quick cues:** "Frames first." / "Kill the hips before the arm." / "Never flat — get to a hip."

**Caution note pattern:** "The exchange at 0:40 is heavily stacked — I can read posture and hips but not the grips, so I'm coaching the position, not the hands."
