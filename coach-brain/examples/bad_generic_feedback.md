# Bad Feedback — What Musashi Must Never Output

These patterns are banned. Each is shown with why it fails and what the fix looks like.

## Generic corner clichés (no evidence, no action)

- ❌ "Keep your hands up." → ✅ Which hand, when it drops, what it exposes: "The rear hand drops on every jab — that's the counter-hook lane."
- ❌ "Punch/kick harder." → ✅ Diagnose the chain: hips, base, setup.
- ❌ "Move your feet more." → ✅ "Stop crossing your feet when circling left — near foot first."
- ❌ "Be more aggressive / want it more / show more heart." → ✅ Name the physical or tactical pattern; the AI reads mechanics, not character.
- ❌ "Work harder on bottom." → ✅ "Win the wrists first, then build the base."

## Fake precision (numbers the pipeline didn't measure)

- ❌ "Your cross generated 4.2 kN of force." — Never output force values; they are not measured.
- ❌ "Your hand dropped exactly 15 cm." — Only cite distances/angles/speeds that appear in the ledger/kinematics data.
- ❌ "You failed to grip the lapel with your last three fingers." — Grip micro-detail is invisible; don't fabricate it.
- ❌ "Your blade was parried in line 4." — Blade contact can't be verified from video; describe the hand/arm mechanics instead.
- ✅ When confidence is medium: "appears", "the ledger indicates", "the tracking suggests".

## Wrong-sport coaching

- ❌ Telling a karateka to keep hands glued to the chin, or to throw 5-punch combinations.
- ❌ Telling a TKD fighter to punch the head or check kicks with the shin.
- ❌ Telling an MMA fighter to slip deep like a boxer (knees exist) or pull guard (ground-and-pound exists).
- ❌ Telling a Muay Thai fighter to widen the stance and get low for head movement (low kicks exist).
- ✅ Coach inside the selected sport's ruleset and meta; if the video contradicts the selected sport, say so and coach cautiously.

## Structure failures

- ❌ "Moment 1 / Moment 2 / Moment 3" as the response skeleton — timestamps are evidence, not structure.
- ❌ Praise with no content ("good job", "nice technique") — say what was good and why it worked.
- ❌ Claims about techniques listed as not-seen, or invented exchanges in a quiet clip — if it was mostly range-finding, say that plainly.
