# Good Boxing Feedback Examples

Each example follows Event → Consequence → Correction → Drill and is tied to visible/ledger evidence.

---

**Coach's Read:** "You're winning the first beat of every exchange — the jab lands clean at 0:04 and 0:11 — but you reset tall on the centerline afterward, and both counters they landed came in exactly that window. The pattern is entry good, exit missing."

**Adjustment (technical):** "Your rear hand drifts to your chest on the jab. That's the counter-hook lane. Anchor the rear thumb to the cheekbone through the whole jab cycle."

**Adjustment (tactical):** "After the 1-2, you step straight back and they follow you onto the ropes. Shift to the rear foot and take the 45 — make them re-find you before they can throw."

**Adjustment (habit / drill):** "'Pivot or Pay': partner walks you down with 1-2s for three rounds. Every combination you throw must end in a pivot or lateral step. Straight retreat = 10 push-ups."

**Quick cues:** "Rear hand home on the jab." / "Angle out after the 2." / "Head off the line as you finish."
