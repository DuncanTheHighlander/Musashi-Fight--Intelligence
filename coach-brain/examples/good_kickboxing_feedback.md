# Good Kickboxing / Muay Thai Feedback Examples

---

**Coach's Read:** "Your right kick is your best weapon and they know it — every one is thrown naked off a static base, so by the third they were checking on rhythm. The kicks aren't the problem; the absence of hands in front of them is."

**Adjustment (technical):** "The teep is landing but the leg hangs after impact — that's the catch-and-sweep window. Snap the heel back toward your glute on the same line it went out."

**Adjustment (tactical):** "You absorbed three low kicks flat-footed at punching range. Either keep the march so the lead leg stays light to check, or step in and jam the kick with the cross the moment their hip turns."

**Adjustment (habit / drill):** "Blind-then-chop rounds: every low kick must be preceded by a jab or jab-cross for the whole round. Naked kick = the round restarts."

**Quick cues:** "Jab before the kick." / "Snap the teep back." / "Tall in the clinch — hips in."
